How-to recipes, step-by-step

Rice

1

When selecting your serving, keep in mind that depending on the type of rice it may expand

2

The commonly used Basmatic can get 5x bigger!

3

With a sieve, clean your rice under running tap water, this will get rid of white powder that makes the rice gummy

4

To see if you have clearned enough, place the rice into a pan or another dark container and add water, if the water turns cloudy you need to keep cleaning

5

Once water is clear, rice is ready to cook, add enough water so your fingertip is covered

6

take rice out, place water in stove, add salt and get it to boil. Basmati rice needs quite a bit of salt

7

Once water is boiling, add rice, turn heat to medium so it is sizzingly. Leave to cook for about 10min
