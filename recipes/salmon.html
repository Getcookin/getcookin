Salmon

1 salmon fillet
salt
1 tbsp oil
1 clove garlic

1. Ensure your salmon is at room temperature
2. Add a pinch of salt to both sides of the salmon
3. On the skin-side, hold both ends of the salmon while it is flat, and push them together to create a slight bulge. With a knife, slice 3 lines on the skin (not too deep), this will allow the salmon to cook more evenly
4. Add oil to pan, heat on medium-low
5. Slowly place salmon onto pan, skin down first
6. Leave salmon to cook for about 3min, watch as you will see it gradually change colour
7. flip salmon slowly once the colour change has occured up to half-way on the steak
8. Add garlic
8. Leave to cook for about 5 more min, without moving salmon * PRO TIP: You don't want it to break apart!
